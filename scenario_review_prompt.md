### **Core Principle**

Accumulate does **not magically automate decisions** or remove human oversight.
Instead, it creates a **cryptographically verifiable trust layer** that:

• Documents who did what, when, and under what authority
• Enables provable delegation and multi-party authorization
• Provides instant auditability
• Enables cross-organizational trust
• Reduces friction and delay in coordination
• Maintains full traceability
• Supports governance continuity when personnel change
• Strengthens compliance, oversight, and accountability

It **improves decision velocity**, but does not eliminate regulatory or procedural steps.
